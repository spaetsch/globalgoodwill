Let's change the world together

Surplus Connection exists to make critical connections between corporate surplus donors, international shippers, and legitamate non-profit organizations in order to ease global poverty. Our process is simple. Whether you are a corporation, a shipping company, or a non profit, you will be connected with opportunities to make a difference.

$13 billion in goods are being offloaded each year. Think of the good we could do together.
